Trial Deposits

Before we are able to transfer funds from your external account, we’ll need to make some trial deposits to that account to verify you are the Account Owner.

Here’s how it works:

  • We’ll make two small trial deposits into your external bank account (such as $0.01 and $0.06).
  • Check your external account to verify the deposits are there. You’ll see them as credits.
  • Sign In .
  • Return to the External Accounts page within 14 days and enter the trial deposit amounts to enable secure transfers between your and your external bank account.
